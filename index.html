Helping others with your computer

A computer is a vital tool for anyone living in this day and age. This is why we aim to donate every 1 in 10 computers we collect to charitable and worthy causes.

Safe & Secure

We ensure all previously stored information on any of the systems we collect is either removed, given to you or destroyed.

Eco-Friendly

We aim to reuse all that we collect from you. Reducing wastage and the manufacture of new systems has a range of benefit for our futures on this planet.

Helping Others

We donate 1 in 10 the machines to charities and worthy causes. This helps grow individuals who are in need of the benefits a computer can bring.

Welcome to We Collect Your IT

We offer a free collection service across the UK for the removal of your old IT systems. We safely resuse this equiptment by ensuring any sensitive or personal information that was on these machines is either physically removed or destroyed before we reuse them. Using us shall not only benefit the environment by avoiding the waste and disposal of this equiptment but shall also help others, as we donate and proportion all of the equiptment we collect.

Why waste? We will collect....

The environmental and social benefits of reuse do not only help conserve natural resources, but help reduce air and water pollution caused by hazardous disposal. Reuse reduces the amount of greenhouse gas emmissions caused by the manufacturing of new products. It simply makes good sense to let us collect and reuse your equipment
